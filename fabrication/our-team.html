Our Team

Meet the key members of ALARI fabrication team.

B.T Yoon

Owner builder

Mr. Yoon has 20+ years of real estate construction experience building custom homes, improving commercial buildings and developing new restaurant and night club venues. Mr. Yoon was born and resides in Honolulu where he is raising his young son.

Ryan Magpantay

Philippine based Senior Architect

Licensed Architect with 17 years of experience in design, construction and project management. Returning to Manila in 2013, Ryan had spent seven years residing and working in Saudi Arabia, spearheading both interior and exterior design with Arab villas and mansions with the Saudi firm: Soha Al Faisal Architecture and Interior Design Group in the city of Al Khobar. Ryan took projects from concept to finish construction in interior design and exterior design of Arab villas and mansions during his tenure at Soha Al Faisal. While Ryan produces all shop drawings (and 3D drawings) from our offices in Manila, he also travels weekly to Cebu where he manages the staffing and production of all fabrication as well as contributes with Christoph to our quality control.

Christoph Wiegand

Wood and Joinery Specialist
Managing Director of Euro Forest Products Industries, Inc.
Product Developer for WHERHOFF blocks

Christoph Wiegand graduated in 1983 from Fachschule Holz University (technical institute) in Stuttgart, Germany where he obtained a Master’s Degree in FURNITURE MAKING and INTERIOR DESIGN. Commissioned out of Germany by the governments of Papua New Guinea, Indonesia, Vietnam and finally the Philippines, – spanning more than three decades, - Christoph consulted and advised the product development, engineering and technical training for expanding the furniture fabrication industries within these nations. Christoph eventually settled in Cebu, Philippines where he is now married with a family. Christoph is the primary importer of European hardwoods into the Philippines – and owns and runs a saw mill and large kiln drying operation. Christoph is one of the highest qualified Master fabricators on fine wood in the country of the Philippines and advises and oversees every ALARI order, beginning from the selection of wood/lumber to the final sanding and packaging of items for shipment.

Celso Manatad
